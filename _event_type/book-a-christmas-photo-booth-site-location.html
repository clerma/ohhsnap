Why Add a Photo/Video Activation to Your Campaign?

Amplified Brand Visibility

Custom wraps, step-and-repeats, and branded overlays keep your logo in every share. Optional branded microsites extend the experience post-event.

Lead Capture & Opt-Ins

Collect names, emails, and opt-ins with compliant forms. Export clean data and measure performance with post-event reports.

On-Site Prints & Instant Sharing

Premium 4×6 takeaways where desired, plus instant SMS/email delivery to drive real-time reach—no QR codes required.

Seamless Integration

Sleek, space-aware builds for expo aisles, lounges, pavilions, and sponsor zones. Fast setup/strike to match tight show schedules.

Studio-Quality UGC

Sponsor-Ready & Insured

COI available on request. Professional attendants, branded uniforms, and reliable show-floor support for flawless execution.
