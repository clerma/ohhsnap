Partnerships with Purpose

At Ohh Snap, generosity is part of who we are. Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of partnering with local schools, churches, non-profits, and community organizations to create moments that matter. Whether it’s helping raise funds, celebrate milestones, or bring joy to those who serve others — we’re here for it.

That said, we receive many donation requests throughout the year. While we wish we could say “yes” to all of them, our goal is to invest where we can make the most meaningful impact. This page explains how we partner with organizations, what we consider when reviewing requests, and how you can apply for donation or discounted support.

Ohh Snap for the Community

How We Give Back and Partner

Discounted Activations

We offer reduced-rate photo booth rentals for non-profits and schools to make professional experiences more accessible while still honoring the time and resources of our team.

Sponsorship Partnerships

We help connect sponsors with your event by offering co-branded booths, prints, and digital galleries — maximizing visibility for all involved.

Donation Opportunities

Occasionally, we donate a booth package for causes that deeply resonate with our team or community mission. These are limited but heartfelt.

Collaborative Fundraisers

We love helping organizations brainstorm creative ways to generate revenue, like branded photo downloads, keepsake prints, or shared sponsorships.

Creative Alternatives

Even if a full donation isn’t possible, we’ll do our best to offer alternative solutions that help you achieve your event goals.

Annual Review Process

We reserve a limited number of giving opportunities each year. Applications are reviewed monthly based on timing, fit, and availability.

Before You Request Support

We know how much heart and effort goes into planning community events, and we appreciate every organization that reaches out. To keep our giving sustainable, we review each request using three guiding principles:

  • Alignment: We prioritize causes that make a local impact — especially those that give back to children, families, or the greater Lafayette and surrounding communities.
  • Engagement: We look for ways to help you not only capture photos, but grow awareness, increase attendance, and connect with donors or sponsors.
  • Stewardship: Every booth requires time, staffing, and equipment. Even discounted or donated events represent a significant cost. We partner with organizations who value and respect that investment.

If you’re planning an event that aligns with our mission, we’d love to explore how we can help — whether that’s a fully sponsored activation, partial discount, or creative partnership that benefits everyone involved.

Ready to Apply?

Submit a Donation or Discount Request

Tell us about your event, your organization, and the impact you’re making. We’ll review your submission and follow up with next steps.
